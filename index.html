About us

Expressive finishes

Everest Colour Ltd. is one of the Paint manufacturing company established on 2068 B.S. (2011 A.D.) in Nepal; involving of young and dynamic professionals from the same trade with different paints company. From 2068 B.S. (2011 A.D.) to this day, in a brief span of time, particularly insignificant in the life of an industry and yet within this short period, it has emerged as a fore runner in Nepal's paint industry. Everest Colour Ltd. with various research and development (R & D) with modern technology provides its better quality products in the Nepalese paint market. The unit manufactures various Exterior and Interior Emulsion paints and primers, Enamel paints, Wood and metal primers, Acrylic Distempers, Aluminum paints, gold paints, Cement paint, Floor paint and Industrial Paints and so on.
